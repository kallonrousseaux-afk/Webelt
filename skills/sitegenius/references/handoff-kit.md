# Client Handoff Kit — template

**Why**: the wow of the animation fades in a week; what makes the invoice feel justified afterward is the client knowing exactly what they own, what works, and who to call. Fill this template for every real client delivery and send it WITH the deploy package. Plain language — the client is not technical.

**Rules**: no invented facts, no jargon (say "your contact form" not "the Supabase POST endpoint"), every `TODO:` from the build appears here with an owner (RNR or client), and the demo-vs-real line is explicit — a client discovering on their own that "checkout doesn't work" is a trust failure even when it was never sold.
