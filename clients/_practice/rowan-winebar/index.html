Neighborhood Wine Bar & Kitchen

A small room, a short list, and very good bread.

Rowan & Vine is a 28-seat wine bar in the West End — natural wine on the list, seasonal plates from a wood oven, and a room built to sit in for a while.

Kitchen Note

"The menu changes with what our farmers bring us — sometimes weekly, sometimes mid-service. We don't chase seasons; we cook what's actually on the doorstep. Bread & butter is always on."
